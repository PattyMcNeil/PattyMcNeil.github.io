About

For over 20 years, I have had the privilege of helping people navigate some of the most difficult situations and challenges they have faced. I was able to do this through my career in Navy Law Enforcement and Security, where I learned the importance of leadership, accountability, communication, and teamwork. Although it is not always viewed as a customer-focused profession, I took pride in striving to ensure outcomes that placed those involved in a position to improve themselves and grow from the experience. Throughout my career, I have developed strong skills in leadership, conflict resolution, interpersonal communication, investigations, and problem-solving in real-world environments. After retiring from the Navy in 2022, I continued building on those skills in the cybersecurity field while working with DefendEdge as a SOC Team Lead and Tier II Analyst. In this role, I gained experience in security operations, incident response, vulnerability management, compliance support, threat analysis, customer communication, and security governance while supporting multiple clients and mentoring team members. I am also continuing to strengthen my technical background through formal education in Software Development at ECPI University, where I am studying programming, databases, networking, Linux, and cybersecurity concepts. Alongside my education and hands-on experience, I continue expanding my knowledge of security technologies, automation, and best practices within the cyber security field. Cybersecurity has become more than just a profession for me — it is something I genuinely enjoy and take pride in. My background in investigations, security operations, leadership, and problem-solving has transitioned naturally into helping organizations protect their systems, users, and data while building stronger teams and processes. I value being part of an environment where teamwork, communication, and continuous improvement are important. My goal is to continue growing within the cybersecurity field while contributing experience, leadership, and a strong willingness to learn and adapt. I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how I can become a valuable member of your team and contribute my experience, leadership, and passion for cybersecurity. Please feel free to reach out here or contact me directly at neil.mclean81@gmail.com.
